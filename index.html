About Me

I’m a detail‑oriented Business Systems Analyst and Agile project lead with 8+ years delivering enterprise e‑commerce initiatives, third‑party integrations, and product data operations. I translate business needs into clear BRDs/FSDs and actionable user stories, facilitate Agile ceremonies, and partner with engineering, product, legal, accounting, and merchandising to ship dependable, scalable solutions.

At FlexShopper (Nov 2023 – Present), I managed the end‑to‑end integration of Logicbroker to power the company’s marketplace—our largest initiative at the time—conducted stakeholder workshops, authored the BRD and JIRA tickets, onboarded 35+ wholesalers/retailers, and integrated with NetSuite to streamline vendor remittance. Post‑launch, I served as SME/de facto Product Owner, improving vendor onboarding through automation and process changes. I also lead merchandising projects including Waterfall financing onboarding for retail partners, category‑specific microsites under the FlexShopper brand, and a Catalog‑as‑a‑Service program (with revenue‑share design and MSA drafting).

Previously at Pet Supermarket, I acted as Product Owner for the Syndigo Riversand PIM, scaling operations to 14k+ SKUs and 200+ vendor onboardings, and delivered integrations with SFCC, Feedonomics, and Bazaarvoice. I value crisp documentation, clean processes, and predictable delivery.
