Logicbroker ⇄ FlexShopper Custom Order/Lease & Accounting Systems

BA/PM · Integration · EDI & APIs

Led integration of Logicbroker with FlexShopper’s custom order/lease management systems and accounting databases (GCP & NetSuite). Delivered multi-vendor onboarding flows, custom architecture for non-standard vendors, and special invoice handling requirements. Produced BRD with system architecture diagram (Visio) and SOPs in Confluence for merchandising, accounting, and operations.

  • Impact: Cut vendor onboarding time by 50%, doubling capacity; reduced manual touches in accounting reconciliation.
  • Stack: Logicbroker (Azure, API-driven), NetSuite, GCP, EDI transactions, dashboards.
  • Artifacts: BRD, Visio system architecture, Confluence SOPs, Go-live Checklist.
  • Complexity: Supported vendors unable to onboard to Logicbroker; implemented custom invoice processing logic.

Catalog-as-a-Service (CaaS) + Revenue Share Process

BA/PM · Integration · Accounting & Partner Systems

Led BA and PM efforts to enable FlexShopper to stream product catalogs to merchant partner websites via a new back-end revenue share model. Gathered and defined requirements leveraging expertise in accounting workflows and custom CaaS applications. Coordinated with third-party financial waterfall partners to integrate lender data for automated invoicing.

  • Impact: Opened a new recurring revenue channel by enabling automated product catalog syndication to partner merchant sites under a revenue-share model
  • Complexity: Designed revenue share process to handle all return scenarios per vendor policy, payment method, and profitability tied to lease origination date.
  • Artifacts: Detailed Visio diagram mapping every revenue share scenario, systems, and processes — served as the source of truth for the Master Service Agreement (co-authored with partner).
