About Me

I’m a detail-oriented Business Systems Analyst with 8+ years of experience delivering enterprise technology initiatives across e-commerce, product data operations, and third-party integrations. I specialize in translating business needs into clear BRDs/FSDs and actionable user stories, facilitating Agile ceremonies, and partnering with engineering, product, legal, accounting, and merchandising to ship work that matters.

Recently at FlexShopper, I led requirements and Scrum activities for a custom back-end rebate system, coordinated the Logicbroker + NetSuite integration to automate vendor remittance and onboarded 35+ vendors, and helped launch category microsites with integrated finance waterfall functionality. Earlier at Pet Supermarket, I served as product owner for the Syndigo Riversand PIM, scaled operations to 14k+ SKUs and 200+ vendor onboardings, and drove integrations with SFCC, Feedonomics, and Bazaarvoice. I love clean processes, crisp documentation, and predictable delivery.
